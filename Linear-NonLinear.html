LINEAR PIPELINING &
NON-LINEAR PIPELINING....

LINEAR PIPELINING

*it is also called as static pipeline as it performs fixed functions.
* output is always produced from the last block.
* has linear connections.
*allows easy functional partitioning.
* generates a single reservation table.
* a series of processors are connected together in a serial manner
* data flows from the first block to the final block of processor.

NON-LINEAR PIPELINING

*is also called as dynamic pipeline as it performs different functions.
*The output is not necessarily produced from the last block.
*has feedback and feed-forward connections.
*Functional partitioning is difficult in non-linear pipeline.
* generate more than one reservation table.
* different pipelines are present at different stages.
*the functions are dynamically assigned.
